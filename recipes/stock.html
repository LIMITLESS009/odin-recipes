Basic Vegetable Stock

This is a very simple way for making vegetable stock which is a great way to use up old vegetables and not let them go to waste

Ingredients

This dish will consist of the following ingredients and portions

  • 2 large carrots
  • 2 stalks celery, including some leaves
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 sprigs fresh parsley
  • 6 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 large bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 quarts water

Steps for this recipe

  • Step 1

    Chop carrot, celery, and onion into 1-inch chunks.

  • Step 2

    Heat oil in a soup pot over high heat.
    Add carrots, celery, onion, scallions, garlic, parsley, thyme, and bay leaves.
    Cook, stirring frequently, until vegetables soften and begin to brown, 5 to 10 minutes.

  • Step 3

    Add salt and water and bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer, uncovered, 30 minutes.

  • Step 4

    Strain. Discard vegetables.
